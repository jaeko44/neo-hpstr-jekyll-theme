Your Questions Answered

What kind of virtualization do you use?
We use KVM virtualization. This type of virtualization has been well proven to perform and is production ready.
Can I get a trial period to test your software?
Yes, you can launch the application without paying - if the invoice isn't paid within 15 days, your license will be terminated.
Where can I host the cloud?
Yes, you can unpackage our software on-premise or off-premise if you'd like.
What can I host in my cloud?
You can offer prepackaged applications inside Virtual Machines, standard Virtual Machines, Docker Containers, Object Storage, and more.
What are the applications you support?
You can find a list of our applications under our Documentation.
Is it possible to install my own OS?
Yes. With KVM it is possible to mount OS installation media (CD/DVD/ISO image) and make it visible as a VPS local CD/DVD ROM. After that the installation will proceed as usual.
Do you offer IPv6 support?
Yes. Our software supports IPv6 networking & private networking.
What is self-healing technology?
Self-Healing technology is a combination of multiple layers of high availability that will ensure 100% uptime with correct setups, the layers include HA & Data Redundancy.
Do you allow editing of reverse DNS records?
Yes, we allow custom reverse DNS entries for your virtual machine IPs. You can update reverse DNS information directly from your control panel.
